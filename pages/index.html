WELCOME TO
FLGS FINDER

A new tool, designed to help you locate your local FLGS (Friendly Local Gaming Stores)!

Finding your FLGS lets you find all the newest games, cards and models you need for your gaming hobby.

As the tabletop gaming hobby grows it helps to give your business to the local shops that help bring your hobby to life. Local stores can help you find the game of your choice, with expert advice and recomendations to your gaming preferences, all while helping your local community and the gaming industry as a whole. Browse our map today and find what local spots are in your area!
